Main Skills:

Community Management, Web Marketing.

  • Google Analytics
  • Google Adwords

Description:

I've always been into social media, creating pages, blogs and fan sites since the very start ! So it was natural for me to think " Hey maybe I should make a Facebook page about it ! " when I spend an entire shift ( Bartender ) hating particularly rude clients and that's when things took off !

Le Barman Vous Deteste started more as a podcast than a fan page, but after a few episodes, people seemed to enjoy the memes a lot more than any video or audio that I would throw there way, so I decided to focus on that and It worked well ! With over 50 000 people on the page now, I'm very happy with it, It doesnt take much effort to post a meme a day or a funny picture and the followers come at an average pace of 200 - 300 people a day, which is great !

When people asked me what I was going to do with this page I never really knew what to say but I still think that growing a crowd is always a good idea, who knows when I'm going to need 50 000 people ? I've got my own little army and I love it.
